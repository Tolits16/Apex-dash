// Objective

Reach the finish line as fast as possible. Hit speed boost pads (yellow) to surge ahead. Avoid red obstacles — they slow you down. Your ghost records every run; beat your own best time to top the leaderboard.

// Features

What Makes It Tick

01
Ghost System

Your best run is replayed as a translucent ghost car. Race against yourself and shave milliseconds every attempt.

02
Speed Boosts

Yellow boost pads scattered across the track grant a burst of speed. Learn which ones to hit for the optimal line.

03
Obstacle Hazards

Red barrels and tyre walls punish sloppy cornering with a significant speed penalty. Precision wins races.

04
Live Timer

Millisecond-precision timer tracks your run. Sector splits show exactly where you're losing time.

05
Leaderboard

Top 5 times are saved locally. A persistent record of glory — or shame — for every session.

06
Tight Track Design

A hand-crafted circuit with chicanes, hairpins, and a long straight. Every corner has an ideal racing line.
